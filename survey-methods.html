Political Survey Methodology

38 minutes
Published: September 28, 2023

This tutorial provides a comprehensive overview of survey methods in political science research, covering:

  • Sampling techniques for political surveys (probability vs. non-probability)
  • Questionnaire design for political topics
  • Measurement of political attitudes and behaviors
  • Common survey modes (phone, online, face-to-face) and their implications
  • Weighting and adjusting survey data
  • Assessing and improving survey quality

Includes examples from election polls, public opinion research, and policy feedback surveys.
