Regression Analysis in Political Science

45 minutes
Published: October 15, 2023

This tutorial covers the fundamentals of regression analysis as applied to political science research. You'll learn how to:

  • Formulate political research questions suitable for regression analysis
  • Prepare and clean political datasets for regression modeling
  • Interpret coefficients for both continuous and categorical predictors
  • Assess model fit and diagnose potential problems
  • Present results in ways accessible to political science audiences

The tutorial uses real-world examples from voting behavior research, policy analysis, and international relations.
